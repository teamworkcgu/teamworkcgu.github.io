ABOUT THE PROJECT

We are the CGUnion team, a group of undergraduates from Taiwan Chang Gung University attending to the annual biomolecular design competition, BIOMOD.

The link you see above is a 3 minute video explaining our project which we spare no effort over the last three and half months in different labs. We aimed to establish a platform by detection of platelet integrin αIIbβ3 as a model, and further expanded for exosome containing cancer markers detection.

  • Nano-Magnetic Beads synthesis
  • Antibodies or recombinant proteins conjugated on Nano-Magnetic Beads
  • Sensitivity comparison between BluBox and western blot analysis
